Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Flickpause ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services and applications.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media account (e.g., TikTok, YouTube) through our application, we may access:

  • Your basic profile information (display name, avatar)
  • Permission to upload content on your behalf

We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to provide our services.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Distribute meditation and mindfulness content to social media platforms
  • Manage content uploads and publishing
  • Improve our content based on aggregated, anonymized analytics

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We only interact with third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) as authorized by you during the account connection process.

5. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored locally and securely. We do not store your passwords. You can revoke access at any time through the respective platform's settings.

6. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your social media accounts at any time
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Contact us with questions about your data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at flickpause@gmail.com.
